Frequently Asked Questions

What is the entry fee to the water park?

The entry fee is $150 per person.

Children 2-10 and audults over 65:$100

What are the opening hours?

We are open from Monday to Sunday 9:00 am to 7:00pm.

Is the cost of cabin and guest room inclusive of free access to the water park ?

No, the cabin prices exclude the cost of water park admissions which are an additional $100 per person.

Is the entry fee all inclusive to free access to the water park ?

Yes, all rides and activities within the park are free.

Where is Harry's water park Located?

#160 Tabaquite Road, Rio Claro , Trinidad and Tobago

How to place reservations for cabins?

You can contact or book with us at 798-0049 ,466-9748 or 392-48-29 . Where our friendly staff will assist your accommodations or enquiries .

Is there free and secure parking ?

Yes , parking is free for water park goers.Free and secure parking is also available for persons booking with cabins.

Can I bring my own food and beverages ?

Yes, you are welcome to bring food and beverages .Our food court is also available and the convenience store is also readily available for any purchases for your needs.

Is there a place to shower and change when i'm ready to leave the park ?

We offer changing rooms and shower facilities in our main both rooms located in the center of the water park.

Do you have any packages for families or groups?

Early arrival groups of 15 and more person's get a free shed.

Groups of 20 and more people's entry fee is $120.00.

Are there accessible amenities for disabled or wheelchair person's ?

Yes there are amenities for wheelchair disabled persons such as free parking, bathroom facilities and entrance accessible.
